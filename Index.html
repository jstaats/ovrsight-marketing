The Platform
COMPLETE VISIBILITY.
FROM FIRST LEAD TO CLOSED DEAL.

Ovrsight.ai gives your management team and your sales floor everything they need to work every lead with precision — without adding headcount or complexity.

🧠
AI Lead Scoring — Know Who to Call Before They Do
Every lead in your pipeline receives an AI-generated priority score based on behavioral signals, lead source, engagement history, recency, and inventory interest. Your team starts each day knowing exactly where to focus — not guessing.
Scores update automatically as leads engage with your website, respond to outreach, or as new interactions are logged. The hotter a lead gets, the higher they rise — and your salespeople are alerted immediately.
🛡️
Safety Net — No Lead Left Behind
Ovrsight monitors every inbound call, form submission, and chat. If a lead isn't logged within a defined window, the system flags it and logs it automatically. Your pipeline stays clean and complete — without relying on anyone to remember.
🔔
Real-Time Pipeline Alerts
When a prospect returns to your website, views specific inventory, or re-engages after going quiet — your salesperson receives an instant alert with full context. Who they are, what they're looking at, and what to say next.
🚗
Inventory-Matched Outreach
When a price changes or new inventory arrives that matches what a prospect was shopping for, Ovrsight automatically sends a personalized notification. Relevant, timely communication — delivered without lifting a finger.
📊
GM Command Center
A single dashboard built for ownership and management. See your full pipeline at a glance — which leads are being worked, which are at risk, which salespeople need attention, and where deals are stalling. Run a tighter operation with full visibility.
🔗
CRM Integration — Works With What You Already Have
Ovrsight connects directly to VinSolutions, DealerSocket, Elead, DealerTrack, and more. Lead data flows in automatically. Scores and insights flow back. Your team keeps working in the tools they know — Ovrsight works quietly in the background, making everything smarter.
